Lentil Dahl

Lentil Dahl is a south asian dish made from stewed lentils and warming spices. It's very easy to make, affordable and delicious. Read below to find out more:

Ingredients:

To make a basic Lentil Dahl you will need:

  • A small onion, diced
  • One bag of red lentils(other variety work as well)
  • Fresh Garlic, finely diced
  • Fresh Ginger, mined or grated
  • Garam Masala
  • Curry Paste
  • Coconut milk, 1 can
  • Diced Tomatoes, fresh or canned
  • Vegetable Broth, 1-2 cups depending on amount of lentils
  • Warming spices, like cinnamon, clove, allspice, cumin, chilli powder, coriander
  • Oil of your choice, I use olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a lightly oiled hot pan, add diced onion and cook until transluscent
  2. Add aromatics, garlic, ginger, garam masala, curry paste, and spices. Cook until it begins to brown
  3. Add Diced tomatoes and cook for 1 minute before adding coconut milk, broth, and lentils. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to a simmer and cover until lentils have hydrated.
  5. Simmer until lentils are tender and the consistency is stew-like. If there is excess liquid you can remove the lid and stir occsionally until reduced.
  6. Can be served with a squeeze of lemon or lime, yogurt, cilantro, or other toppings of your choice. I like it served over coconut rice.
