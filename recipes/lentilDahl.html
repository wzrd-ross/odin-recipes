Lentil Dahl

home

Lentil Dahl is a south asian dish made from stewed lentils and warming spices. It's very easy to make, affordable and delicious. Read below to find out more:

Ingredients:

To make a basic Lentil Dahl you will need:

  • A small onion, diced
  • One bag of red lentils(other variety work as well)
  • Fresh Garlic, finely diced
  • Fresh Ginger, mined or grated
  • Garam Masala
  • Curry Paste
  • Coconut milk, 1 can
  • Diced Tomatoes, fresh or canned
  • Warming spices, like cinnamon, clove, allspice, cumin, chilli powder, coriander
  • Oil of your choice, I use olive oil
