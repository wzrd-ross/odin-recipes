The Reuben is a sandwich on marbled rye with corned beef and sauerkraut -- plus a few surprises! Read below for more:

Ingredients:

To make the Reuben you will need:

  • Marbled Rye
  • Corned Beef
  • Sauerkraut
  • Thousand Island Dressing
  • Swiss Cheese
  • You could mix it up with a few more things like tomatoes or pickles, but the sandwich is already perfect-- so why bother?

Steps:

  1. In a lightly greased pan, add the corned beef, sauerkraut
  2. In another pan, place two slices of buttered marbled rye
  3. Once your beef and kraut have browned slightly on one side, flip to the other and place swiss cheese on top.
  4. When your cheese has melted and your bread has browned, spread thousand island dressing over the bread and and your beef and kraut.
  5. Place the entire constructed sandwich in the pan with a lid over it, then flip, one minute on each side.
